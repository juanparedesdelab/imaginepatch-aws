The Celestial Night Patch is part of our limited edition Mystic Skies series — hand-embroidered by skilled artisans using 12-weight rayon thread for vivid, long-lasting color. The deep navy felt base is cut to a rounded square with slightly softened corners, giving it a premium sewn-on look even when iron-applied.


Perfect for personalising jean jackets, backpacks, hats, totes, or anywhere that needs a touch of magic. Each patch ships on a branded card backing with application instructions included.

  • 100% premium felt backing (navy, 2mm thickness)
  • 12-weight rayon embroidery thread — colourfast & fade-resistant
  • Iron-on heat-activated adhesive pre-applied
  • Machine washable — turn garment inside out, cold wash gentle cycle
  • Do not tumble dry or iron directly over patch after application
  • For extra hold: sew around the border after ironing
